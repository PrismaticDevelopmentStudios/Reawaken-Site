About

What is Reawaken?

Reawaken was founded by a survivor of a Traumatic Brain Injury, Matt. Severely injured in 2019 he was unable to work for 3 years. When he brgan appying for jobs after 2 years of recovering, no companies would hire him, inluding coffee shops that he applied to. In order to compensate for the large gap on his resume, Matt enrolled in a web development sourse offered by the University of Denver. Matt has met many survivors and many end up getting fired because of the deficits caused by their TBIs.

Matt decided that one way he could help get Tbi survivors back into the workforce was by creating a nonprofit coffee shop that hired TBI survivors. And so Reawken was born. A place for TBI surivors to work and to host meetings for TBI survivors and for anyone needing a safe space to meet. And so Reawaken was born. The name "reawaken" has two meanings. We serve coffee, which can help one to reawaken after a night's sleep. But also, many TBI survivors were in comas and had to rake up from their comas and recover through extensive tharapies until they are recovered enough to work again.

We all need a little help waking up sometimes.
