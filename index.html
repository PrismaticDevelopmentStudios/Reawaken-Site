Coffee & Brains?

At Reawaken we serve coffee, pastries, and sandwiches. But wait it gets better. Our dounder created this nonprofit coffee shop to create a business that hires Traumatic Brain Injury (TBI) survivors, like himself. Often getting a TBI makes one unable to work for for many months, if not years. Then it becomes much more difficult getting hired because of the gap on ones resume. Not only that, but often TBI survivors have struggles with memory which can cause problems at work, and often lead to TBI survivors getting fired. Reawaken employs TBI survivors aith an understanding iof heir potential stuggles and hardships that may make is hard to stay employed.
